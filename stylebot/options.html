    Sync (experimental)

    This will sync your custom styles across multiple computers. For this to work, you need to have Bookmark Sync enabled.


    Important: When you click on Push Styles Now, your custom styles on this computer will be pushed to and replace existing styles on all other computers. This is until automatic sync is built into Stylebot.
